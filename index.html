CCCI
Cross Country Carbon Inflation Calculator


Checking inflation rates in different countries helps you see how well each country is doing economically and whether it's a good place to invest money in or travel. 

However, sustainable investing has become a significant trend. Investors increasingly favour countries and companies that demonstrate a commitment to reducing carbon emissions. Assessing a country's carbon emissions can be integral to aligning investments with long-term sustainability goals.

This website allows the users to interact with the data and compare how the purchasing power changed over time in GB, FR, DE and IT, compare countries’ inflation performance and correlate it with carbon emissions that year.

KEY FUTURE DEVELOPMENT: Countries report CO2 emissions based on accounting, i.e. check national production across all sectors and multiply with typical carbon emissions. However, more activities are done using renewable power or grid mix power and accounting is not able to cater for this change at a national level. Satellite sensor technology adopted by space agencies can overcome this by measuring sunlight’s interaction with the Earth’s atmosphere.

Sensors and satellite network connectivity can connect human activity data with the environment and help make more accurate decisions.
